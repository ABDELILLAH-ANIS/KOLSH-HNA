## 3. Billing Cycles / دورات الفوترة

### Monthly / شهري
- Pay monthly, activate monthly.
- Suitable for merchants who want to try before committing.

### Annual / سنوي — وفّر شهرين!
- **Pay for 10 months, get 12 months** (equivalent to 2 free months).
- Annual plan is recommended for established merchants.
- Annual payment is made in full upfront.

## 4. How to Pay / كيفية الدفع

### Payment Method
> **All payments are manual via WhatsApp.** There is no online payment gateway currently.

### Payment Steps / خطوات الدفع
1. Choose your desired plan during registration at `signup.html`.
2. After registration, your account status is **PENDING (معلق)**.
3. A **WhatsApp button** appears — click it. It pre-fills a message with your email/Gmail.
4. Send the message to the admin and indicate which plan you chose.
5. Admin will respond with payment instructions (bank transfer, CCP, BaridiMob, or cash).
6. Send your **payment proof** (screenshot or receipt) to admin via WhatsApp.
7. Admin verifies and **activates your account within 24 hours**.

### Accepted Payment Methods / وسائل الدفع المقبولة
- 🏦 Bank Transfer (CPA, BNA, BEA, etc.)
- 📮 CCP (Chèque Postal — La Poste Algérie)
- 📱 BaridiMob (Barid Bank mobile app)
- 💵 Cash (by arrangement with admin)

> For payment details and account numbers, contact admin via WhatsApp. Details are not published publicly for security reasons.

## 5. How to Upgrade Your Plan / كيفية الترقية للباقة الأعلى

### Upgrade Process / خطوات الترقية
1. Log into your merchant dashboard.
2. Go to **Settings / الإعدادات** → **Subscription / الاشتراك**.
3. Click **Upgrade Plan / ترقية الباقة**.
4. The system automatically contacts the admin or opens a WhatsApp chat.
5. Agree on the **price difference** (prorated or full, depending on timing).
6. Pay the difference and send proof to admin.
7. Admin upgrades your plan — new features unlock immediately.

### Downgrade
- Downgrades are possible at renewal time only (not mid-cycle).
- Contact admin via WhatsApp to arrange a downgrade before your next billing date.

## 7. Frequently Asked Pricing Questions / أسئلة شائعة حول الأسعار

**Q: هل هناك عمولة على كل عملية بيع؟**
A: لا. كلش هنا لا تأخذ أي عمولة على المبيعات. تدفع فقط رسوم الاشتراك الثابتة.

**Q: Is there a free trial?**
A: There is no free trial currently. Contact admin for a demo or consultation.

**Q: Can I pause my subscription?**
A: Subscriptions cannot be paused. If you stop paying, the account moves to SUSPENDED status after expiry.

**Q: ماذا يحدث لو انتهى اشتراكي؟**
A: يتم تعليق المتجر تلقائياً. لديك 7 أيام للتجديد قبل أن يُخفى متجرك من أمام الزبائن.

**Q: هل يمكنني تغيير الباقة في أي وقت؟**
A: الترقية: ممكنة في أي وقت. التخفيض: عند التجديد فقط.

**Q: هل الأسعار قابلة للتفاوض؟**
A: الأسعار ثابتة لكن تواصل مع الإدارة للعروض الخاصة والمتاجر الكبيرة.
