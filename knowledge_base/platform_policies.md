## 1. Subscription Terms / شروط الاشتراك

### 1.1 General Terms
- All subscriptions on Kolch Hna are **paid in advance** before account activation.
- Subscriptions are available in **monthly** and **annual** billing cycles.
- Annual subscriptions benefit from a discount equivalent to **2 months free**.
- The subscription period begins **on the date the admin activates the account**, not the date of payment.
- Each subscription is tied to **one merchant store only**. A merchant wanting multiple stores must subscribe separately for each.

### 1.2 Available Plans / الباقات المتاحة
- **Basic** — للبداية المتواضعة
- **Gold** — للنمو التجاري
- **Premium** — للمحترفين
- **VIP** — للمتاجر الكبرى والموزعين

> See `pricing.md` for full plan feature comparison.

### 1.3 Subscription Renewal / التجديد
- Merchants are responsible for renewing their subscription before expiry.
- The admin will send a reminder via WhatsApp **7 days** before expiry when possible.
- Expired accounts are moved to **SUSPENDED** status automatically.
- A suspended merchant has a **grace period of 7 days** to renew before data archival.
- After the grace period, the store listing is hidden from customers. Store data is retained for **30 days** then may be permanently deleted.

## 2. Account Activation Process / إجراء تفعيل الحساب

### Step-by-Step Activation
1. Merchant completes registration on `signup.html` — account status becomes **PENDING**.
2. Merchant clicks the **WhatsApp button** displayed after registration, which pre-fills a message containing their **Gmail / email address**.
3. Merchant sends their **payment proof** (screenshot or transfer confirmation) to the admin via WhatsApp.
4. Admin verifies the payment manually.
5. Admin activates the account — status changes to **ACTIVE**.
6. Merchant receives confirmation and can log in to access their dashboard.

### Activation Timeline
- Normal activation: **within 24 hours** on business days (9:00–17:00, Algeria GMT+1).
- Weekends / public holidays: activation may take up to **48 hours**.

### Important Notes
- Accounts that remain in **PENDING** state for more than **7 days** without payment confirmation may be deleted.
- The admin email is: **bourekanis@gmail.com**
- All payment and activation communication goes through **WhatsApp** (number provided by admin on the platform).

## 3. Refund Policy / سياسة الاسترجاع

### No Refund After Activation
- **كلش هنا لا تقدم أي استرجاع للمبالغ بعد تفعيل الحساب.**
- Kolch Hna does **NOT offer refunds** once the merchant account has been activated.
- This policy applies to all plans and billing cycles (monthly and annual).

### Exceptions
- If payment was made but account was **never activated** due to an admin error, a full refund or free activation extension can be negotiated.
- Technical issues preventing store functionality must be reported within **48 hours** to qualify for possible compensation (e.g., free days added).
- All refund/compensation requests must be sent to: **bourekanis@gmail.com** or via WhatsApp support.

### Before You Subscribe
- Merchants are encouraged to review plan features, demo stores, and contact support before paying.
- Free consultations are available via the AI agent on the platform or direct WhatsApp.

## 4. Account Suspension Rules / قواعد تعليق الحساب

### Reasons for Suspension
An account may be suspended for any of the following reasons:

| Reason | Immediate? |
|--------|-----------|
| Subscription expired | Yes (automated) |
| Selling prohibited goods (see acceptable use policy) | Yes |
| Fraudulent activity or fake orders | Yes |
| Customer complaints verified by admin | After investigation |
| Providing false business information at registration | Yes |
| Repeated non-response to customer orders | After warning |

### Suspension Process
1. Admin sends a **warning via WhatsApp/email** (except for immediate violations).
2. Merchant has **48 hours** to respond and rectify.
3. If unresolved, account is suspended — store hidden from public.
4. Merchant may appeal by contacting: **bourekanis@gmail.com**.

### Reinstatement
- Accounts suspended for expiry can be reinstated immediately upon payment renewal.
- Accounts suspended for policy violations require admin review before reinstatement.

## 5. Data Privacy Basics / أساسيات خصوصية البيانات

### Data Collected
- **Merchant data**: name, business name, phone, email, store details, subscription info.
- **Customer data**: name, phone number, wilaya, address (for delivery).
- **Order data**: product names, quantities, prices, delivery status.

### Data Usage
- Merchant data is used solely for account management and platform communication.
- Customer order data is shared with the merchant fulfilling the order and the delivery partner (Yalidine Express / Z-Express).
- Kolch Hna does **NOT** sell or share customer or merchant data with third parties for marketing purposes.

### Data Retention
- Active account data is retained as long as the subscription is valid.
- After account deletion, data is retained for **30 days** then permanently erased.

### Customer Rights
- Customers may request deletion of their personal data by contacting: **info@kolchhna.com**
- Merchants are responsible for their own compliance with customer data they collect outside the platform.

## 6. Acceptable Use Policy / سياسة الاستخدام المقبول

### Prohibited Products / المنتجات الممنوعة
Merchants must NOT list or sell:
- Alcohol, narcotics, or controlled substances
- Weapons, ammunition, or explosive materials
- Counterfeit or pirated goods
- Pornographic or adult content
- Products that infringe on trademarks or intellectual property
- Any item illegal under Algerian law

### Prohibited Behaviors / السلوكيات الممنوعة
- Creating fake orders or manipulating the platform metrics
- Harassment of customers or other merchants
- Spamming customers with unsolicited messages
- Misrepresenting products (false descriptions, wrong photos)
- Using the platform to collect payments outside the merchant-customer relationship (scams)

### Merchant Responsibilities
- Merchants are **fully responsible** for the accuracy of product listings.
- Merchants must respond to customer inquiries within a **reasonable time** (recommended: within 24 hours).
- Merchants must honor confirmed orders unless there is a stock issue (which must be communicated immediately).

## 7. Platform Fees Structure / هيكل رسوم المنصة

### Fee Model
Kolch Hna operates on a **flat subscription fee model**. There are **NO per-transaction commissions**.

| Fee Type | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Transaction commission | 0% — None |
| Subscription fee | Per plan (see pricing.md) |
| Payment gateway fee | None (manual payment via WhatsApp) |
| Delivery fee | Charged by Yalidine / Z-Express directly to merchant |

### What the Subscription Covers
- Hosting of the merchant store page
- Product listing slots (varies by plan)
- Order management system
- WhatsApp integration for orders
- Access to shipping integrations (Yalidine / Z-Express API)
- Analytics dashboard
- VIP plan: Ad module + priority support + up to 8 product images

### What Is NOT Included
- Delivery costs (paid directly to carrier)
- Custom domain (not currently offered)
- Ad design services (available on request from admin — separate pricing)
